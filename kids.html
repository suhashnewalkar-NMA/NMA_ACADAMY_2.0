About The Program

We don't just teach kids how to kick and punch; we teach them how to be leaders. Our program helps children build confidence, deal with bullies non-violently, and develop a lifelong love for fitness. Classes are split by age and skill level to ensure safety.

What You Will Learn:

  • Practical Anti-bullying strategies
  • Focus and listening skills games
  • Coordination, balance, and motor skills
  • Respect for parents, teachers, and peers
