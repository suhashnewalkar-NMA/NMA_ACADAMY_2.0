Submission Grappling

No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu. Submissions. Control.

About The Program

Submission Grappling is the ground-fighting aspect of MMA without the traditional uniform (Gi). It focuses on using leverage and technique to submit opponents via chokes and joint locks. It is fast-paced, highly technical, and essential for self-defense.

What You Will Learn:

  • Submission hunting (Chokes, Armbars, Leg locks)
  • Positional escapes and reversals
  • Guard passing and retention
  • Functional flexibility
